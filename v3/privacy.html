Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you interact with our website, including when you:

The personal information collected may include:

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Job title
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

We may also collect certain information automatically when you visit the website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system

This information helps us understand how visitors use our website and allows us to improve functionality and user experience.

We may use the information we collect for business purposes, including to:

  • Respond to inquiries and contact requests

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our website

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve the browsing experience.

Cookies may be used to:

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

We may use trusted third-party service providers to support the operation of our website and business operations.

These may include providers of:

These third parties only receive the information necessary to perform their services and are expected to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your personal information.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:
